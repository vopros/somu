Fuckyeah
========

The initial implementation of Fuckyeah belongs to [Artёm Polikarpov](http://github.com/artpolikarpov/fuckyeah). I’ve [ported it](http://github.com/malfermu/fuckyeah) to Ruby 1.9.

Works out-of-the-box with Rails 3 and Sinatra.
Scales. Got a simple-to-use API:

```ruby
require 'fuckyeah'
all = Dir["posts/*"].fuckyeah
```

Under the [WTFPL](http://www.wtfpl.net/).
Install and use in your projects:

```shell
gem install fuckyeah
```
